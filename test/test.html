Our Journey

Key milestones in our mission to create positive change

  • 2015: Foundation Established

    Dugra Foundation was established with a focus on education and healthcare in underserved communities.

  • 2016: First Education Program

    Launched our first education center serving 50 children from rural communities.

  • 2018: Healthcare Initiative

    Established our healthcare program with mobile medical camps reaching 1,000+ people.

  • 2020: Green Earth Campaign

    Launched our environmental sustainability program, planting 5,000 trees in the first year.

  • 2022: National Recognition

    Received the National Community Service Award for our contributions to social welfare.

  • 2024: Global Expansion

    Expanded our programs to neighboring countries, creating an international network of support.
